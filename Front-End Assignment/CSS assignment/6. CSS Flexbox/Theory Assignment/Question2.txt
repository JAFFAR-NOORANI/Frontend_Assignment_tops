Question 2: Describe the properties justify-content, align-items, and flex-direction used in Flexbox.

Ans : 

The main Key Flexbox Properties are 

1) justify-content: It helps to Aligns items horizontally along the main axis. 
Its Value are
flex-start (default): Items align at the start.
flex-end: Items align at the end.
center: Items center horizontally.
space-between: Even spacing between items.
space-around: Equal spacing around items.


The main Key align-items properties are to vertically along the cross axis
Its Values are :
stretch (default): Items stretch to fill.
flex-start: Items align at the top.
flex-end: Items align at the bottom.
center: Items center vertically.


The main porpose of  flex-direction is to Defines the direction of the main axis.
Its Values are :
row (default): Items flow left to right.
row-reverse: Items flow right to left.
column: Items flow top to bottom.
column-reverse: Items flow bottom to top.
